NUTRASSIST

This is a Google UX design certificate project created with a focus on social good. The NutrAssist concept was designed as a nutritional coaching service for users who are new to technology. I conducted user research, created wireframes, prototypes, and mockups, and designed both a responsive website and a native mobile app. The responsive website focuses on straightforward nutrition goals and easy access to healthy recipes and nutrition tips, while the native app was designed as a nutrition tracker to help users pursue their individual goals.

Key challenges I faced while creating this project were researching and designing the best ways to easily present nutritional coaching to people who were new to technology with graphs and charts. Research studies indicated that users preferred their daily nutritional data displayed on the homepage of the app, while the responsive website provided recipes and nutritional tips according to their individual health needs.
