Creativity, a relentless quest for knowledge, and a passion for user solutions are the driving force behind my design voyage.

Greetings! I am a San Diego based freelance designer with 15 years experience in illustration and graphic design and 1 year of experience in UX design, web design and front end development in Webflow and native app design in Figma. I am also relatively new to working with 3D in Blender and excited to learn more every chance I get. I am certified by Google in UX design.

I find inspiration all around and utilize it in my design work. The space between design solutions and artistic creativity is the world where I exist.

If I'm not busy working on design, I'm likely improving my culinary skills, producing music, or catching a Premier League or NBA game. As a relative newcomer to web and UX design, my portfolio is modest but growing constantly. I am hungry to expand my design skills and provide client satisfaction by creating user solutions. Design rules everything around me. Let's get to work and make your vision a reality.
