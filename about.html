ABOUT

My main focus is UX / UI design and front end web development, but I also have a background in graphic design. I'm certified by Google in UX Design and have a specialized interest in visual and interaction design.
“Good design is as little design as possible” - Dieter Rams’ 10th principle. This principle consistently informs my design decisions. I believe in providing solutions that directly address user pain points.
If I'm not working on design projects, I'm likely improving my culinary skills, creating music, or catching a Premiere League or NBA game.
