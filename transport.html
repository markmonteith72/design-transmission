TRANSPORT

This is a Google UX design certificate project created for a fictional music venue native mobile app. The Transport app design was created with a focus on a shopping flow for users to purchase event tickets and venue merchandise. In addition to designing the mobile app, I also conducted user research, and created wireframes, prototypes, and mockups.
